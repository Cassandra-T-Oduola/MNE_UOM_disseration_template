\begin{declaration}
\justify
Plagiarism is defined as “the unacknowledged use, as one’s own work, of work of
another person, whether or not such work has been published” (Regulations
Governing Conduct at Examinations, 1997, Regulation 1 (viii), University of Malta).\\

\vspace{1em}
\justify
I, the undersigned, declare that this thesis submitted is my work, except where
acknowledged and referenced.\\
\vspace{1em}

\justify
I understand that the penalties for making a false declaration may include, but are not limited to, loss of marks; cancellation of examination results; enforced suspension of studies; or expulsion from the degree program.\\
\vspace{1em}

\justify
Work submitted without this signed declaration will not be corrected, and will be given zero marks.\\
